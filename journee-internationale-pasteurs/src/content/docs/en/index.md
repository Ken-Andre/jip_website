## Honoring those who watch over our souls

A spiritual celebration bringing together pastors, bishops, apostles and Christian leaders from Africa to :

- **Honor** freely the pastoral ministry
- **Reflect** on spiritual impact in Africa
- **Connect** generations of spiritual leaders
