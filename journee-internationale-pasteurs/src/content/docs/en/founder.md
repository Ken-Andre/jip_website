# Pastor Patrick - Founder of JIP

Pastor Patrick is the visionary man behind the International Day of Pastors. With over 20 years of experience in pastoral ministry, he deeply understands the challenges and joys of pastoral service.

## His Story

Pastor Patrick began his ministry in a small rural church in Cameroon. There, he realized the isolation that pastors can sometimes feel, especially in rural areas.

## His Vision

One day, while praying for his team of pastors, the vision of JIP was revealed to him: to create a special day to honor and encourage pastors all over the world.

> *"Honoring those who watch over our souls"*

This simple but powerful phrase has become the motto of JIP.

## His Impact

- Founder of 3 successful churches in Cameroon
- Mentor to over 100 pastors
- International speaker on pastoral leadership
- Author of several books on ministry

## His Mission

Pastor Patrick firmly believes that pastors deserve more recognition and support. That's why he has dedicated his life to creating JIP and building a worldwide community of united pastors.
