# Our Vision

The International Day of Pastors (JIP) was born from a clear and inspired vision: **honoring those who watch over our souls**.

## Our Mission

We firmly believe that pastors play a fundamental role in our communities. This special day is dedicated to them for:

- ✅ **Recognizing their devotion** and daily sacrifice
- ✅ **Providing continuous training** adapted to current challenges
- ✅ **Creating a worldwide community** of supportive pastors
- ✅ **Encouraging excellence** in pastoral ministry

## Our Values

- **Excellence**: We aim for quality in everything we do
- **Solidarity**: Together, we are stronger
- **Training**: Continuous learning is essential
- **Inspiration**: We want to rekindle the flame of ministry

## Our Impact

We aspire to transform the lives of pastors and, by extension, their communities through:

- Quality training sessions
- Authentic fellowship moments
- Practical ministry resources
- International support network
